Wishlist

Here at DART, we have many needs to help us better serve the community during disaster related events. Below are a list of items that we have put together that we need and use during such events. We also have an Amazon affiliate link where we have created a wishlist as well. All items can be brought to the Putnam County Emergency Managment Agency at 700 County Services Dr. Cookeville, Tennessee 38501

  • Gift Cards (Walmart, Dollar Tree, Lowes, Amazon, Harbour Freight, Tractor Supply, Petsmart, Sporting Goods Stores)
  • Inflatable Mattress
  • Walkie Talkies (not toy versions)
  • Gas Cans
  • Water hose, Watering Cans (Red, Blue, & Black)
  • Hose Splitters
  • Crates
  • Clip Boards (Need 100+), 3x3 Dry Erase Boards/Markers
  • Files
  • "S" Hooks
  • Leashes (Dog & Horse leeds), Clip Leads, & Collars
  • Food and Water Dishes
  • Pooper Scooper & Bags
  • Potty Pads
  • Paint Scrapers (non-plastic)
  • Work Gloves
  • Folding Tables and Chairs
  • Power Washer (Electric)
  • Extension Cables
  • Portable Charging Packs
  • Tool Boxes
  • Spray Bottles
  • Cattle Ear Tags for mark crates with
  • Bleach
  • Zip Ties
  • Aluminum Pans
  • Coolers

We also have some items that are not needed at this moment due to very generous sponsors and community members.

  • Pine Sol
  • Clorox Wipes
  • Paper Towels
  • Other cleaning supplies that are not stated above
